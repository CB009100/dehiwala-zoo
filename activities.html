ACTIVITIES

Sea Lion Show

The Sea Lion show is one of the main attractions of the zoo and takes place every day in a special location near the entrance of the Aquarium, where visitors will be able to witness the performance of “Chakku” the Sea Lion. Chakku is a very intelligent creature that has been trained to entertain the crowd by performing a number of different tricks, which includes balancing a ball on top of his nose, jumping through a hoop and various other acrobatics.

Price : LKR 3000.00

Time : 4.00pm

Meet The Elephants

“Meet the Elephants” is a great opportunity for you to get up close and personal with our beloved Elephants. You will be able to experience the elephants in action right in front of your eyes and learn about their behavior and how our zookeepers take care of them.

Price : LKR 5000.00

Time : 4.30 pm

Night Safari

Specific areas of the zoo are open to observe nocturnal animals. Birds and reptiles that are active at night are brought out to the elephant show area where the staff explains their behavior patterns. The reptile section and aquarium are also open during these late hours. At the entrance of the zoo is fish therapy which costs around LKR 200.00 for a 15-minute session per person. People are also given the option to experience the night safari by hiring golf buggies that could carry up to a group of 8 people.

Open : Friday - Sunday

Time : 7pm - 10pm
